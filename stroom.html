Empathize

This project began with a meeting with the client to discuss the requirements and create an outline of what should be on each page.

Define

Based on the insights gathered in the empathy stage, the following statement was created, "How might we create a website that effectively communicates the company's values and offerings to potential customers in an innovative manner?‚Äù from there I started sketching wireframes.

Ideate

At this stage of the project, my focus was on developing the visual lay-out and creating wireframes. I started looking at similair companies and their websites and looking for innovative web design. I began sketching out ideas for the website and combining those element to create a compelling wireframe. The wireframe helped me to visualize the website's layout, structure, and content, and served as a starting point for further refinement and iteration.

The final wireframe

Prototyping

During this phase of the project, my primary focus was on creating the website, selecting the appropriate colours and fonts, and designing the elements from the wireframe. Drawing on my design creativity, I worked hard to bring the website to life, ensuring that it accurately reflected the company's visual identity and values, as well as being innovative while remaining consistent with the company's identity.It took several iterations and versions to arrive at the current design.

Testing

In the final stage of the project, we conducted thorough testing of the website design to ensure that it met the project's objectives and effectively communicated the company offerings to the target audience. Using a range of testing methodologies, we identified areas for improvement and iteratively refined the design to enhance the user experience and drive engagement.
