Wordpress,UI/UX Project

This project involved the development of a comprehensive website that effectively showcases the services provided by the company.

View website

Empathize

This project commenced with conducting an in-depth analysis of the current website by engaging both existing and potential customers through interviews. The aim was to identify any existing issues or shortcomings in the website's design and functionality.

Define

During this phase, I commenced cataloging the identified problems to formulate a comprehensive and distinctive problem statement for each category.

Ideate

In this phase, I generated diverse ideas and layouts for the website, drawing inspiration from similar services and examining the recommendations and issues identified in the previous phase.

Prototyping

I started this phase by making paper prototypes and then testing them to get a clear idea of what I wanted the website to look like and then building it in wordpress.

Testing

During the final phase, the website was subjected to additional testing to evaluate the clarity and understandability of the company's services. Any necessary adjustments were made to optimize the website's functionality and user experience.
