Empathize

This project commenced by conducting interviews with our target audience to determine which features for a customer portal would align most effectively with the customers of the company.

Define

Based on the insights gathered in the empathy stage, the problem statement was created, "How might we create a website design for a informaion portal that effectively aligns with the customers wishes?‚Äù from there we started brainstorming design and ways to embed the features for the portal

Ideate

At this stage of the project, my focus was on developing a clear designs with the visual identity of the brand, and creating the best user flow by having brainstorming sessions,creating flow charts and started skecthing .

Prototyping

In this phase of the project, my primary focus was on finalizing the design and building the website, using the insights and designs developed in the previous stages to shape the prototype. Drawing upon my technical skills in web development and my creativity in design, I worked diligently to bring the website to life, ensuring that it accurately reflected the brand's visual identity and values.

Testing

In the final stage of the project, we conducted thorough testing of the website design to ensure that it met the project's objectives and effectively communicated the brand's values and offerings to the target audience. Using a range of testing methodologies, we identified areas for improvement and iteratively refined the design to enhance the user experience and drive engagement.
